The biggest gaps to fix first

Ranked by patient impact + how often they bite us.

1

Patient self-booking after the sleep test

Sequence 3 sends "time to book" but the booking is a phone call. Drop a real scheduling link (Calendly, Acuity, NexHealth) into Sequence 3 and the bottleneck disappears.

2

Tag-setting depends on staff remembering

The whole chain breaks if nobody manually sets test-completed, consultation-complete, appliance-purchased, appliance-delivered. Each missed tag = a sequence that never fires.

3

Shipping notifications

From "kit ordered" to "kit arrived" the patient gets nothing. Two automated emails (label-printed + tracking-update) would kill a lot of "where's my test?" calls.

4

Appointment confirmations & reminders

Once a consult is on the calendar, no reminder emails or texts go out. Adds avoidable no-shows.

5

Google review request after delivery

Need to confirm Sequence 5 actually asks for one. If not, it's the easiest layup in this whole document.
